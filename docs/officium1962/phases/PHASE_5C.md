# Phase 5C Summary: Tempora Scripture

Date: 2026-07-20

Status: complete.

The parser classifies Scripture readings from resolved Divinum Officium output and records likely `Tempora` source families in sourceRefs. Annual progression and transfer details remain resolved by the pinned Perl engine rather than recalculated in TypeScript.

Fixture coverage includes Lent, Passiontide, Advent, Christmas, Eastertide, ordinary Sundays and weekdays, and leap day material.

Verification is included in the Matutinum fixture manifest and oracle comparison.
